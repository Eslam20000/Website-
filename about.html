Our Company

the leading scrap metal recycling company in Saudi Arabia.

Metals Recycling Co. Ltd. established in 2006 and now we are the leading scrap metal recycling company in the kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We purchase scrap steel materials both local and overseas. We use heavy equipment’s, machineries and manual labor to sort the scrap into various metals classifications and produce recycled scrap metal products so it can be used to manufacture new metal products. We also resell a portion of the scrap metals we purchased without processing if it meets our customer’s requirements.

The company maintains its dedication to produce a high quality of shredded materials and scrap metal products that meets our customer’s quality standards. Our main goal is to deliver quality product and services for our customer’s satisfaction.
